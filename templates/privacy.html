Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 18-03-2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to FeedFusion. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our service, including data obtained via the Instagram API and Facebook Login.

2. Information We Collect

When you log in using Facebook Login, we may collect and store the following information with your consent:

  • Public profile information (name, profile picture, etc.)
  • Email address (if permission is granted)
  • Other authorized data required for app functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Authenticate users and provide access to app features
  • Personalize user experience based on Facebook profile data
  • Ensure compliance with Facebook's Platform Policies

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share limited data with third-party services that help us operate our app in compliance with Facebook and Instagram API policies.

5. Data Security

We follow industry best practices to protect your data, including encryption and secure storage. We do not store sensitive authentication details.

6. User Control and Data Removal

You have the right to request data deletion. To remove your data from our system, please visit Facebook App Settings or contact us at support@feedfusion.com.

7. Contact Information

If you have any concerns regarding privacy, please contact us at support@feedfusion.com.
